About Uttarakhand 360

Uttarakhand 360 is a community-driven platform created to provide reliable weather information, travel guidance, road updates, pilgrimage information, trekking conditions, and local resources across Uttarakhand.

Our mission is to make planning safer and easier for residents, tourists, trekkers, and pilgrims by bringing essential information together in one place.

The platform will continue to grow with features such as road status, Char Dham updates, AI travel assistance, weather alerts, and destination guides.
