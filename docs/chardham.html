🛕 Char Dham Yatra

The journey begins with faith. The mountains decide the rest.

Travel with devotion. Travel with respect. Travel prepared.

🙏 Before You Begin

Char Dham is one of India's most sacred pilgrimage circuits. It is much more than a tourist destination. Please respect local traditions, temple customs, the Himalayan environment, and fellow pilgrims.


Leave only footprints, carry back only memories, and let the mountains remain as beautiful as you found them. 🙏

🛕 About the Char Dham

District Rudraprayag

One of the twelve Jyotirlingas dedicated to Lord Shiva.

District Chamoli

One of the holiest temples dedicated to Lord Vishnu.

🎒 Pilgrimage Essentials

Carry With You

  • 🥾 Comfortable Trek Shoes
  • 🧥 Warm Jacket
  • 🌧 Raincoat / Poncho
  • 🧤 Woollen Cap & Gloves
  • 💧 Water Bottle
  • 🍫 Light Snacks
  • 💊 Personal Medicines
  • 🔋 Power Bank

Helpful Reminder

Mountain weather changes rapidly. Even on a bright morning, always carry warm clothing and rain protection.

🙏 Pilgrim Tips

Travel Responsibly

  • 🙏 Respect temple traditions.
  • 🗑 Keep the Himalayas clean.
  • 🤝 Respect fellow pilgrims.
  • 🚭 Avoid smoking inside temple premises.
  • 🌿 Protect nature and wildlife.

A Friendly Reminder

The mountains reward patience. Walk slowly, stay hydrated, and allow your body to adjust to the altitude.

😊 If someone says, "बस पाँच मिनट और..." prepare for another twenty.

🚑 Emergency Contacts

Emergency Numbers

🚑 Ambulance — 108

🚓 Police — 112

🔥 Fire — 101

📞 Disaster Helpline (State emergency numbers may vary.)

Travel Safely

Always check official weather forecasts and road conditions before starting your journey. Avoid travelling during heavy rainfall or official advisories.
