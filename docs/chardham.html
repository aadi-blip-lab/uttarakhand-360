🛕 Char Dham Yatra

The journey begins with faith. The mountains decide the rest.

Travel prepared. Travel responsibly. Travel with devotion.

🙏 Before You Begin

Char Dham is one of India's most sacred pilgrimage circuits. It is much more than a tourist destination. Please help preserve its spiritual atmosphere, respect local customs, follow temple traditions, avoid littering, and remember that the Himalayas are both beautiful and fragile.


🙏 May your journey be safe, peaceful and spiritually fulfilling.

🎒 Pilgrimage Essentials

Carry With You

  • 🥾 Comfortable Trek Shoes
  • 🧥 Warm Jacket
  • 🌧 Raincoat / Poncho
  • 🧤 Woollen Cap & Gloves
  • 💧 Water Bottle
  • 🍫 Light Snacks
  • 💊 Personal Medicines
  • 🔋 Power Bank

Helpful Reminder

Mountain weather can change within minutes. Even on sunny mornings, always carry warm clothing and rain protection.

🙏 Pilgrim Tips

Travel Responsibly

  • 🙏 Respect temple traditions.
  • 🗑 Do not litter.
  • 🚭 Avoid smoking inside temple areas.
  • 🤝 Respect fellow pilgrims.
  • 🌿 Protect the Himalayan environment.

A Gentle Reminder

The mountains reward patience. Walk at your own pace, stay hydrated, and give your body time to adjust to higher altitudes.

😊 If someone says, "Bas 5 minute aur..." expect another 20.

🚑 Emergency Contacts

Emergency Numbers

🚑 Ambulance — 108

🚓 Police — 112

🔥 Fire — 101

📞 Disaster Helpline (State numbers may vary)

Safety First

Always check official weather and road updates before beginning your journey. Avoid travelling during heavy rainfall or official advisories.
